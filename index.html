Bosco Model United Nations




Spanning three days, this intellectually stimulating conference houses the Tower of Basel, the Black Hand, the Trilateral Commission and United Nations Security Council. Deliberate on socially-relevant issues which reinforce your understanding of the way the world works. Live in the pulsating veins of history by taking steps that could change the world map, quite literally. Let your performance here serve as an echo for those who have their sights set on a future career in public deliberation and the government. Broaden your horizons by interacting with the young, enterprising members of the closely-knit MUN community. The intricacies of the bustling worldview have been brought to your doorstep. Opening the door is all that's left. What if the whole world looked to you? Would you step up, and inspire?
